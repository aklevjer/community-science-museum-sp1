Discover science magic

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. This is why we have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore.

Many of these exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits – they only come to life at night when everyone’s gone home).

Special exhibitions and events

Nature's wonders

On loan from the BioSphere Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Nature's Wonders exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. Journey through diverse ecosystems, from rainforests to deserts, and discover the wonders of Earth's species.

Visiting professor of aeronautics

Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.

Night in the museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

Mysteries of the ocean

Dive into the secrets of the oceans and the wonders they hold. Explore underwater ecosystems, deep-sea creatures, and the groundbreaking technology that allows us to study the depths.

Admission

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.
