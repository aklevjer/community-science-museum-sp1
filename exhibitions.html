Exhibitions

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.

Biology and medicine

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology are offering us unique insights into the great wonders of the tiny world. Over a series of exhibits we explore the history of medicine and take a look at some major breakthroughs including the discovery of penicillin and the first heart transplant.

Evolution

Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evo-lved our time. You can also take some time to meet a few of the creatures who didn’t survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs too.

Ecology

As we uncover the impact of human activity on our planet, it's crucial for scientists to pave the way toward a sustainable future—for us and the entire Earth. Join us in explor-ing how we can make a positive difference for our world and all its inhabitants.

Robotics and ai

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.

Chemistry

Dive into the world of Chemistry and dis-cover the magic of atoms, molecules, and reactions. Uncover colorful concoctions and mind-bending experiments that shape our everyday lives. Explore the fundamental principles of matter in this captivating exhibition.
